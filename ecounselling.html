Counselling Sessions

A counseling session for students is a dedicated time and space where students can openly express their thoughts , emotions, and concerns in a supportive and confidential environment. These sessions are typically facilitated by trained counselors or professionals who specialize in providing guidance and support to students. During a counseling session, students have the opportunity to discuss various aspects of their lives, such as academic challenges, personal issues, mental health concerns, or career aspirations. The counselor actively listens, offers empathy, and provides guidance to help students gain insight, develop coping strategies, and make informed decisions. Counseling sessions aim to promote personal growth, enhance well-being, and empower students to overcome obstacles, manage stress, and navigate their educational journey more effectively. We provide face-to-face and online sessions according to student interest.
