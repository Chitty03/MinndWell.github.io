Resources We Offer

This resources would help students to communicate easily,

Workshops And Seminars

Workshops and seminars about mental health among students are vital educational events that focus on raising awareness, promoting understanding, and providing practical tools for addressing mental health challenges among students.

Counselling

Counseling sessions for students provide a convenient and accessible way for students to seek support for various academic, emotional, or personal challenges they may be facing.

Discussion Forum

A discussion forum among students is a valuable network that fosters a sense of belonging, offers emotional support, and encourages personal growth through interactions with fellow students who share similar experiences and challenges

Student Advocates

"Student Advocates for Mental Health" is a student-led initiative or organization that works to raise awareness, promote understanding, and advocate for improved mental health support and resources on campus.
